These Subscription Terms govern the paid features of the Wonder Walk mobile application (the “App”). By purchasing a subscription, you agree to these Subscription Terms.

1Subscription Overview

Wonder Walk offers optional paid subscriptions that provide access to premium features.

Subscription options, pricing, and duration will be displayed in the App at the time of purchase.

2Payment

  • Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at the time of purchase
  • All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information

3Auto-Renewal

  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled
  • Your account will be charged within 24 hours before the end of the current billing period
  • The renewal will be at the same price and duration as your original subscription

4Free Trials (if offered)

  • It will automatically convert into a paid subscription unless canceled before the trial ends
  • You will be charged at the end of the trial period if not canceled

5Cancellation

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings
  • Cancellation must be made at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period
  • Deleting the App does not cancel your subscription

After cancellation:

  • Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period
  • You will not be charged for the next period

6Refunds

All purchases are handled by Apple.

  • We do not issue refunds directly
  • Refund requests must be submitted to Apple via the App Store
  • Refund eligibility is determined by Apple’s policies

7Price Changes

We may change subscription prices from time to time.

Any price changes will take effect at the next billing cycle.

8Access and Features

Access to premium features is available only during an active subscription period.

If your subscription expires or is canceled, access to premium features will be removed.

9Changes to Subscription Terms

We may update these Subscription Terms from time to time. Continued use means you accept updates.
