Wonder Walk (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Wonder Walk collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application.

1Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve the app.

a) Personal Information

  • Name (provided during onboarding)

We do not require account creation and do not collect email addresses or other personal identifiers.

b) Health and Fitness Data

  • Step count
  • Walking activity data

With your permission, Wonder Walk may access health-related data from Apple HealthKit. This data is used solely to provide core app functionality, such as tracking your walking activity.

We do not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or data mining purposes.

c) Location Data

With your permission, Wonder Walk collects your location data while you are actively using the app.

  • Track your walking routes
  • Display your movement on the map using Apple Maps

We do not collect location data in the background.

2How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

Provide and maintain app functionality Track walking activity and progress Improve app performance and user experience
We do not use your data for advertising or marketing.

3Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4Subscriptions and Payments

Wonder Walk offers in-app subscriptions processed through Apple’s App Store.

We do not collect or store your payment information. All billing is handled securely by Apple.

5Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide app functionality and comply with legal obligations.

6Your Privacy Choices

You can control your data through your device settings:

  • Health Data: Manage access via Apple Health settings
  • Location Data: Enable or disable location access in iOS Settings

7Children’s Privacy

Wonder Walk is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated “Last Updated” date.
